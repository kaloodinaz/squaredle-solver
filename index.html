About Squaredle Solver

This is a solver for the popular word-finding game, Squaredle.

Please note that the generated results may not exactly match Squaredle's official word list, but they should include nearly all the required answers.

This site is not affiliated with Squaredle in any way.

IMPORTANT: Using this solver to unfairly achieve a perfect score and top the leaderboard is strongly discouraged. Abuse may lead to account suspension from Squaredle's leaderboard.
